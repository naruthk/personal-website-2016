Proposal

My honors thesis (still being revised) is Characterization and Effects of Comments Made on Scratch Coding Platform. My project will seek to analyze how the quantity/type of comments received make a Scratcher more or less likely to comment on others’ projects. In other words, it will be a study of how comments received affect comments left.

Scratch

Developed by MIT and launched in 2002, the Scratch platform is both a programming language and an online community that provides the opportunity for children to program and create interactive content and share them with other Scratch users around the world. By 2017, more than eighteen million users have registered on the Scratch platform. On Scratch, users can create and work on projects and can also post comments. The ability to provide feedback by commenting on other people’s work makes Scratch a social community.

Liverpool Mobile App using Adobe XD

Animated version

Soccer is my passion too and the team that I enjoy cheering is Liverpool FC, a UK based professional soccer team. So I used Adobe XD to sketch out and design a prototype of my Liverpool app's interface.

Interface

Landing Page

When the user launches the application, Bill Shankly's quote shows up. Shankly was one of Liverpool's greatest manager in the 90s who made Liverpool so popular globally. Between his quote and his name is a loading bar that, when fully loaded, will be completely white.

News Page

The user then lands on the News section. By default club news is selected. However, users can tap on the "Filter News" bar to customize.

Matches Page

I think a user wants to see the results of the most recent match first, so that's what is shown at the top. The user can then scroll down to preview other matches.

    • Images:Wikipedia for the club logos, Liverpool Official Website for match photos.
    • Date:May 2017
    • Build with:Adobe XD
